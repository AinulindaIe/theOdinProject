Labneh

Easy to make middle-eastern cheese(ish)

  • 500 ml natural yogurt
  • 10 g salt
  1. Mix yogurt and salt
  2. Pour into fabric bag or coffee filter, that can funnel excess water off
  3. Let drain in the fridge for 1-3 days* - discard any water that drains off

* - The amount of time draining determines the firmness of the labneh. 1 day is good for a spread. 3 days and the labneh can be rolled into balls and stored in oil upto two months
